ReliefMap Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 5, 2026

ReliefMap ("we," "our," or "the app") is a mobile application that helps users locate public restrooms. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Location data. When you use ReliefMap, we request access to your device's location services to show restrooms near you. Your location is used solely to query nearby points of interest and display them on the map. We do not store, log, or transmit your precise location to our servers or any third party, except as described below regarding Google Maps.

Search queries. When you search for a city, address, or landmark within the app, that query is sent to Google's Places API to return results. We do not store your search history on our servers. Recent searches are stored locally on your device only and are not transmitted anywhere.

No account required. ReliefMap does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

No analytics or tracking. We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising trackers, or any third-party tracking tools. We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or usage statistics.

2. Third-Party Services

Google Maps Platform. ReliefMap uses Google Maps SDK and Google Places API to display maps and search for locations. When you use the app, Google may collect certain data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. This may include your approximate location and search queries. We encourage you to review Google's privacy policy for details on how they handle this data.

Apple App Store / Google Play Store. When you purchase ReliefMap ($1.99), the transaction is processed entirely by Apple or Google. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card number, or billing address. Apple and Google handle all purchase-related data under their respective privacy policies.

3. Data Storage

ReliefMap stores minimal data, and only on your device:

  • Your filter preferences (categories, open-now filter, accessibility filter)
  • Recent search locations (up to 5, stored locally)

No data is stored on external servers. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

4. Children's Privacy

ReliefMap does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The app does not require account creation and collects no personal data from any user.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your data with any third parties. The only external service that receives data during normal app use is Google Maps Platform, as described above.

6. Your Rights

Because we don't collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no personal data for us to delete, export, or modify. You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device's Settings app, and the app will continue to function using manual search.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
